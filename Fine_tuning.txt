Finetuning --> Retrain the pretrained Model on a small domain specific dataset.
QLORA, LORA

Supervised FineTuning
1. Collect Data
2. Choose a method --> Full parameter-finetuning LORA/QLORA
3. Train for a few epochs --> Keep the base weights frozen, or partially frozen and update only a small subset (LORA) or full weights (Full Finetuning).
4. Evaluate & Safety-test --> Based on exact-match, factuality.


Cons:
1. Cost the LLM
2. Have to have strong technical expertise
3. Time consumed to train the Model.
